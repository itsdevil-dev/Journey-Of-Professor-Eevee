Glaceon

About Glaceon

Glaceon is an Ice-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. It evolves from Eevee when leveled up near an Ice Rock or exposed to an Ice Stone. Known for its calm demeanor and chilling beauty, Glaceon can freeze the air around it into ice crystals, creating dazzling snowstorms.

Stats

  • HP: 65
  • Attack: 60
  • Defense: 110
  • Sp. Attack: 130
  • Sp. Defense: 95
  • Speed: 65

Evolution Path

Eevee → Glaceon (use Ice Stone or level up near an Ice Rock)

  • Mirror Coat
  • Hail
  • Freeze-Dry

Did You Know?

Glaceon’s body temperature can plummet to sub-zero levels, allowing it to freeze moisture in the air into sharp needles of ice—making it both elegant and powerful in battles.
