Vaporeon - The Bubble Jet Pokémon

About Vaporeon

Vaporeon is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Eevee when exposed to a Water Stone. With its sleek blue body, fish-like tail, and flowing fins, Vaporeon thrives in aquatic environments.

It can melt into water, making it nearly invisible. Vaporeon is admired for its high HP, strong Water-type moves, and its graceful presence.

Stats

  • HP: 130
  • Attack: 65
  • Defense: 60
  • Sp. Atk: 110
  • Sp. Def: 95
  • Speed: 65

Abilities

  • ★ Water Absorb - Heals Vaporeon when hit by Water-type moves.
  • ★ Hydration (Hidden Ability) - Heals status conditions in rain.

Top Moves

  • ★ Hydro Pump - A powerful water cannon blast.
  • ★ Surf - A wave of water crashes down on the foe.
  • ★ Ice Beam - Freezes enemies with chilling precision.
  • ★ Acid Armor - Sharply boosts Vaporeon's defense by liquefying its body.

Habitat & Behavior

Prefers calm lakes and deep seas. Often seen surfing ocean waves alongside trainers. Its body can refract light, making it appear invisible from above water.

Fun Facts

  • Vaporeon’s fins can change shape to mimic aquatic plants for camouflage.
  • It can create miniature water clones to dodge attacks.
  • Legends say it guided sailors safely to shore in stormy seas.
